The darkage of Console Game.

From 1980 to 1995, the global console game sales went up and down. The future of the console game at that time was not clear.



Atari is dead!

Atari was the only game-console at early 80s. However, Atari did not control their game quality and allowed game developers to copy a game and rebrand it.

This finally resulted in Atari Shock, a video game crash happening at 1982 - 1984.

At the end, Atari bankrupted ...

Long live Nintendo!

After this shock, Nintendo took the chance and made a commerical success with three consoles, GB, NES and SNES.

To aviod the Atari Shock, Nintendo was applying a higher tax for games published on NES and SNES. In this way, game developers would put more time on how to imporve game quality.

However, the monoploy position and restrict rule left resistance among game developers ...

Nintendo is evil!

With the high-pressure policy Nintendo were using, more and more game companies started to challenge Nintendo's position.

At that time, tens of game-consoles were in the market. While, none of them were able to take Nintendo down!

However, this open competition did stimulate the whole market ...

New Age

To take down Nintendo's monopoly position, many manufacturers started making their own consoles.

Sony and his PS 1 won the game ...
