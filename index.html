How does console game change in the past 30 years?

From 1980 to 1995, the global console game sales went up and down.

The first major recession is because the Atari Shock, a video game crash happening at 1982 - 1984.

At that time, Atari was the hegemon of the game industry. However, Atari tended to make more low quality games, which eventually destoried Atari itself.

After this shock, Nintendo took the chance and made a commerical success with two consoles, NES and SNES.
